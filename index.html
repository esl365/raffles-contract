Raffles Language Course instructors serve as primary representatives of Raffles Language Course. All instructors are expected to adhere to the following Guidelines & Expectations. Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in termination of the contract.

EN

1. All instructors teach the students to execute the followings:

  • To make Korean students express their thoughts in English well
  • To make Korean students converse with native speakers confidently
  • To make Korean students improve their listening ability and pronunciation better
  • To make Korean students use sophisticated vocabulary in various situations
  • To make Korean students have natural and proper manners in English

1. 모든 강사는 학생들이 다음을 수행하도록 가르칩니다:

  • 한국 학생들이 다양한 상황에서 세련된 어휘를 사용하도록 만들기

E. Contract Termination / E. 계약 해지

1. The contract may be terminated under the following circumstances:

  • If "B" is unable to fulfill the responsibilities outlined in this Employment Agreement, "A" reserves the right to terminate the contract. Similarly, if "A" fails to fulfill its contractual obligations, "B" may terminate the contract.
  • In case that "B" is incapable of understanding Korean culture and doesn't follow "A's" instructions by the reason of cultural differences, "B" will get fired.
  • If B fails to conduct himself in a professional manner, resulting in damages of the image of “A”, "A" has the right to terminate the contract at the time of "A's" notification to "B". (Corrected "B" has the right to "A" has the right based on context)
  • In case that B breaks the law (swindling, illegal private tutoring, rape, violence, larceny, drug, abuse), the contract will result in immediate termination and "B" will be punished by the International Law.
  • "A" will notify "B" beforehand, if "A" intends to cancel the contract for the reason of the difficulties of the management. If “B” seeks release from the contract for personal reasons, "B" has to submit the notice in the written form and "B" must perform the work until a new teacher substitutes “B” at the institute.
  • If "B" turns out to be lack of ability by the director, the head instructor and/or the students' monthly surveys, "A" can dismiss "B.”
KR

1. 다음과 같은 상황에서 계약이 해지될 수 있습니다:
